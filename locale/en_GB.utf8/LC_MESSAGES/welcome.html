Welcome to Ultiorganizer

Ultiorganizer is a scoring system for Ultimate tournaments. Here you can follow schedules, results, standings, teams, players, spirit scores, and statistics.

What do you want to do?

  • Follow the tournament? Browse the links in the left navigation pane. Registration is not required for public event information.
  • See schedules, results, or standings? Open the relevant division or pool page.
  • Check a team or player? Use the Teams pages to open team, club, and player information.
  • View statistics? Browse event, team, player, club, country, and all-time statistics when available.
  • Enter results or Spirit of the Game scores? Use Scorekeeper or Spiritkeeper if you have the appropriate rights.
  • Manage team or event data? If you have a team or organizer role, use the account access options available in this installation.
