Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers the use of this Ultiorganizer installation.

General information

  • The organisation operating this Ultiorganizer installation is responsible for the personal data processed through it.
  • Personal information means information about an identifiable individual.
  • When you create a user account, Ultiorganizer stores account information such as:
    • Name, username, password and e-mail address.
  • Additional profile information, player details, media links, and similar content may be stored when users or administrators add them to the system.
  • Ultiorganizer uses personal information for identification and authentication, account management, service operation, communication, and service improvement.

Logging and IP addresses

When you use the system, technical log data may be stored. This can include your username, IP address, and details of actions that change system data.

  • Diagnosing problems found with the system.
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the service.
  • Investigating misuse or illegal activity.

Information sharing and security

  • Your account information is visible only to authorised users with special access rights to the system, and they are expected to keep that information confidential.
  • Your password is stored only in hashed format and is not visible in plain text.
  • Ultiorganizer does not rent or sell personal information.
  • Personal information may be disclosed when needed to operate the service, when required by law, or when the user has given permission.

Your information

  • You can change your account information at any time.
  • If account deletion or other privacy-related action is needed, contact the system administrator of the installation you are using.
  • Questions about retention periods, legal basis, or local privacy requirements must be answered by the organisation operating this installation.
