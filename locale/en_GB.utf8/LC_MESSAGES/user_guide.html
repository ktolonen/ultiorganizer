Ultiorganizer is a scoring system for Ultimate tournaments. It provides public information such as schedules, results, standings, team and player pages, spirit scores, and statistics. Teams, game officials, and event organizers can also use it to enter and manage tournament data.

  • Follow the tournament - Browse the event as it progresses
    • Schedule and results - View upcoming and past games, scores, and final standings.
    • Divisions and pools - Follow the status of each division and pool through their own pages.
    • Teams and players - Open team, club, and player pages to see rosters, statistics, and game lists.
    • Statistics - If activated, browse statistics from events, players, teams, clubs, countries, and the all-time Hall of Fame.
  • Enter tournament data - Input tools for teams and game officials
    • Scorekeeper - Used to enter results, player lists, and scoresheet details.
    • Spiritkeeper - Used by teams, spirit officials, and organizers to enter or review Spirit of the Game scores.
  • Registered users - Features that require a user account and the appropriate permissions
    • Team representatives and players can manage team, club, and player information when they have the required rights.
    • Registered users can also add media links to supported team, player, club, and gameplay pages.
  • Organizer roles - Tools for event organizers
    • Tournament Director - Can manage the event structure, divisions, pools, teams, scheduling, games, standings, and related event data.
    • Spirit Director - Can manage spirit scoring settings, review Spirit of the Game submissions, and follow missing or incomplete spirit scores.
  • Extra tools - Additional features available on some pages
    • Game history - If teams have played against each other earlier, a history link is shown.
    • iCalendar - Import schedules into calendar services such as Google Calendar.
    • Printing - Print schedules or open PDF versions from the page links.
    • Gameplay - When scoresheets have been entered into the system, gameplay links are shown next to games.
